About Our Enterprise

Joy's furniture, home furnishings retailer that was the world's largest seller of furniture in the early 21st century, operating more than 300 stores around the world. Joy's furniture specializes in low-priced goods, sold whenever possible in compact “flat-pack” form for in-home assembly by the customer.
